🔗 TEST ON YOUR PHONE RIGHT NOW:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Find your computer's IP address:
   Mac: System Preferences → Network
   Windows: cmd → ipconfig
   
   Example: 192.168.1.100

2. On your phone, open browser and go to:
   
   http://192.168.1.100:5173/customer-hub?loan=DEMO-123
   
   (Replace 192.168.1.100 with YOUR IP address)

3. Make sure phone is on SAME WiFi as your computer!

✅ WHAT CUSTOMER DOES:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Taps link in SMS
2. Reviews loan details
3. Uploads licence photos (front & back)
4. Takes verification selfie
5. Accepts terms
6. GPS tracking starts automatically
7. Keeps page open during loan
8. Clicks "End Loan" when returning
